The Chewy

Information and Notes

Prep Time=20min
Inactive Prep Time=1 hour
Cook Time=30min
Serves 2 dozen cookies

The darker the sugar you use, the chewier your cookies will be.

Ingrediants

  • 8 ounces unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces bread flour, 4 ounces all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 ounces granulated sugar
  • 8 ounces light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 ounce whole milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips

Steps To Make Delicious Cookies

  1. Preheat ovn to 350 degress F
  2. Melt butter in microwave, set aside
  3. Sift together flours, salt, and baking soda, set aside
  4. Measure the sugars, set aside
  5. Whisk together the whole egg, the whole egg yolk, milk, and vanilla extract, set aside
  6. Pour flour mixture and sugars into your stand mixer and mix on medium for 2 minutes
  7. Reduce speed and slowly add the egg mixture, mix until thoroughly combined, about 30 seconds
  8. Add the flour slowly and at intervals
