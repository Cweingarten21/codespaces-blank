1. Miracle


Miracle is a movie about the 1980 US olympic hockey team that defeated all odds to win the semifinals against in that time the best team in the world, the Soviets,
and then went on to win the gold medal against Sweden. This movie is a great example of pure determination and not giving up despite the odds.

2. Field of Dreams

Field of Dreams is about a young man in Iowa named Ray Kinsella who build a baseball field on his farm in the hopes that Shoeless Joe Jackson will come back to live.
Not only does Shoeless Joe come back to live but eventually two whole teams come back from the dead. After going on an adventure to find Terrance Man,
Ray finds out that his father had also come back because he played major league baseball for a little while. He and his father have a catch and that is how the movie ends.
