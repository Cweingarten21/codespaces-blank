Links

Links are very useful, and help users to quickly navigate to new pages, both within and outside of the current website.
For instance, the can quickly direct users to Google's Homepage.
Emails can also be easily sent through the use of links.
They can even bring a user to a certian place within a webpage.

Images

Sometimes we want to go ahead and align pictures to the left of a line of text. Using the "align" attribute, with a value of "left" is a perfect way to do this. You could also align the pictures to the right of text by replacing the value in the "align" attribute to "right".
